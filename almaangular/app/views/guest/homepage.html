About Us?

Almanourisher was founded in 2016 with a vision to bridge the nutrition gap among children by empowering appropriate nourishment combined with regular physical activities. We bring in nutrition intervention by engaging and educating the families. We partner with Pre-schools/Schools and help them to structure the nutrition program, create intervention among parents and create a healthy school environment and healthy children.

Our Services

We want what is best for the children and it all begins with good nutrition. It’s a concept designed program that offers a year- long engagement program by partnering with schools and pre-schools. This encourage students and parents to think of their nutritional decisions as health decisions.

Health risk assessment

It is a useful tool that helps parents to keep track about the growth pattern and development of their child.

Dietary assessment

The frequency of consumption of a defined list of foods with portion size helps us estimate the intake of nutrients met by individual child.

Awareness & workshops

It helps to bring in nutrition intervention and awareness on child nutrition, thereby develop healthy food habits that lasts for lifetime.

Teacher workshop

Our teacher workshop encourages teachers to co-create a healthy eating and nourished environment within the schools.

e-consultations

Parents can avail consultation from our nutrition experts through online or telecall consultation.

Menu planning

The primary objective in the school is to create healthy eating environment through right menu planning with simple yet healthy recipes.
