About Us

Almanourisher was founded in 2016 with a vision to bridge the nutrition gap among children by empowering appropriate nourishment combined with regular physical activities. We bring in nutrition intervention by engaging and educating the families. We partner with Pre-schools/Schools and help them to structure the nutrition program, create intervention among parents and create a healthy school environment and healthy children.

Our Purpose

Through our nutrition program, we provide intervention among all the stakeholders that brings a fundamental knowledge on nutrition in children which enhances the nutrition and wellbeing of children.

India…. A country rich in heritage and traditional values, with a high population lacks in appropriate nutritive distribution especially amongst the growing children. Just as each child growth patterns vary, their nutritive requirements & demands vary.

No wonder! Children today are exposed to a lot of temptation from lip-smacking but unhealthy food choices. Just feeding the kids is not enough but as parents, it is our duty to ensure they meet their nutrition adequacy. Their success at schools or on fields highly depends on how healthy they are.

Hence, Kids’ health is a BIG responsibility! As we know good nutrition is the key to:

  • Create a future healthier generation.
  • Enable children accomplish their life goals…

Therefore, we aim to educate families about nutrition and empower children to develop lifelong healthy eating habits with a fun approach. Our phases of service advocate good nutrition by incorporating nutrition intervention and building awareness.

Researches have shown that kids who eat healthy show signs of….

  • Proper growth and development
  • Better immunity
  • Healthy weight
  • Improved vision and skin
  • Improved memory & quick grasping
  • Sleep better
  • Reduced absenteeism in schools
